NDYL stands for New Directions for Your Learning.

This initiative was founded to provide free, practical support for apprentices pursuing digital and technical roles in the UK. Our first focus is on the Level 3 Data Technician (ST0795) standard, helping learners build confidence and skill through structured examples.

We aim to:

  • Break down complex tasks into clear steps
  • Provide practical resources aligned with the IfATE standard
  • Support learners from a wide range of backgrounds and abilities
