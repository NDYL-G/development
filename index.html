Data Technician (ST0795) Apprenticeship Support

This site supports apprentices working toward the Level 3 Data Technician (ST0795) apprenticeship. It is designed for beginners, especially those with limited technical experience, and aligns with the official assessment plan.

Based on the IfATE Data Technician Standard, this site helps you develop the knowledge, skills, and behaviours needed for success in Assessment Method 1: Scenario Demonstrations with Questioning.
