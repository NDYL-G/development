Data Technician (ST0795) Apprenticeship Support

Welcome! This site supports apprentices working toward the Level 3 Data Technician (ST0795) apprenticeship. Whether you're new to spreadsheets or looking to boost your confidence for your EPA, this site walks you through the full data journey: from gathering and cleaning data to analyzing, presenting, and automating it.

This content is based on the official IfATE Data Technician Standard (ST0795) . It specifically focuses on Assessment Method 1: Scenario Demonstrations with Questioning.
